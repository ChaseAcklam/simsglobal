About sims global solutions

Sims Global Solutions Founder Ted Sims knows logistics inside and out. He built his career in shipping from the ground up, and understands its complicated nature. Since launching in 2010, SGS has relied on two core competencies to provide outstanding logistics for its customers: service and simplification.


With a vast and agile system of freight services supported by industry-leading technology, customers rely on and return to Sims Global Solutions for dependable and cost-effective shipping services.


We have built a team of logistics experts all with common goals and mentalities. Our work is not done until the mission is accomplished.


We will fulfill and exceed your expectations every time. That’s our promise.
