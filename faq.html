What’s the difference between Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) and truckload freight?
Weight and size are the key factors between LTL and truckload shipments. For example, cargo that’s small in size but very heavy may require truckload shipping.


Can I track my shipment from my phone?
Our site is mobile-friendly, but quoting and tracking software are best experienced through a desktop, laptop, or tablet. If you prefer regular updates, we are happy to set up email confirmations.


Does Sims use their own fleet of trucks?
Instead of owning and operating our own fleet, we decided from the beginning to reduce our overhead–and your expenses–by building and leveraging our network of top carriers to meet your needs. Combining our service with their operations gives you a cost-effective shipping solution using the fastest and highest-quality transportation.


How can I get a quote?
Receive a free, no-obligation quote for our Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) shipping services by filling out a short quote form. Simply answer a few questions and we’ll be in touch within minutes–not days.


What can I do to make sure I’m getting an accurate quote from the beginning?
When it comes to time-sensitive transportation, no one likes surprises. The more information you can provide about your shipment, the more accurate your quote will be. Understanding your product and the types of shipping methods available will save you time and money, and help give you the edge you need to beat out your competitors.
